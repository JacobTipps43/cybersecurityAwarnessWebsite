Fake Website Example

Fake websites often look almost identical to real ones. Always check the URL carefully, look for misspellings, and verify secure connections (HTTPS). Scammers rely on small details being overlooked.

Text Message Scams

Be cautious of unexpected messages containing links. Scam texts often use urgency, typos, or suspicious email addresses. Never click unknown links or provide personal information.

Phone Call Scam

Scam calls often impersonate trusted organizations. They may use caller ID spoofing or pressure tactics. Never share sensitive information over the phone unless you initiated the call.
